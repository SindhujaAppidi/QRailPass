QRailpass

NextGenTrain

The QRail Pass project introduces a secure travel authorization system for trains, utilizing 12-digit Aadhaar numbers to generate QR codes. These QR codes serve as authorization tokens for passengers. Ticket collectors equipped with QR scanners can efficiently validate passenger identities and travel permissions. This enhances boarding efficiency and safety during emergencies. The project demonstrates the versatility of Aadhaar beyond traditional identification. It envisions a future of streamlined and secure train travel. By merging Aadhaar with QR technology, QRail Pass prioritizes passenger convenience and safety.
